Philosophers Pong



Welcome to the Philosophers Pong!



This is a simple game where you can play against the computer or against a friend.



The game is based on the classic Pong game, but with a twist: the paddles are philosophers!



You can choose between playing against the computer or against a friend. If you choose to play against the computer, you can choose the difficulty level.
